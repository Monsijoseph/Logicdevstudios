Our Story

The journey of LogicDev Studios from passionate beginnings to creating immersive gaming experiences

About LogicDev

LogicDev is a game development studio founded in 2019 by brothers Chibuzor Armstrong Ifedigor and Chinwike Mark Ifedgor, driven by their shared passion for video games.

Over time, the team has expanded to include talented individuals such as Precious Otite Abraham and Victor Nannador Kelly, who have made significant contributions to the studio's growth.

Our Journey

From Humble Beginnings

LogicDev began its journey utilizing Unity Engine for game programming and Blender for 3D development. Since its inception, the studio has worked on various game projects, building expertise and reputation in the industry.

Notable Projects

  • Rear Automatic
  • Quizcastle
  • Escape Protocol
  • OTITE Reborn (current project)

Collaborations & Events

Partnerships

LogicDev has successfully collaborated with Nerdwork to create JaJa Crowns and Chains. The studio has also engaged in freelancing and remote working projects across Nigeria, showcasing its versatility and expertise.

Game Expos

  • Game Expo Porthacourt 2024
  • Gamathon Lagos 2025

Current Project: OTITE Reborn

LogicDev is currently working on OTITE Reborn, an action-packed game set in an African-themed world. This project showcases the studio's commitment to creating engaging and culturally rich gaming experiences.

With a strong foundation and a growing team, LogicDev is poised to continue delivering innovative and exciting games that captivate audiences worldwide.
